-->"Objects":Physical entities with properties (attributes) and behavior (methods).Represented using curly braces {}.
    eg:let x={
        name: "John",
        age: 55
        };

-->Accessing Object Values:
    1.Dot Notation:
        eg:console.log(x.name); //Output:John
    2.Bracket Notation(Index Signature):
        eg:console.log(x['name']); //Output:John
